Our Story

Churn & Chill began with a simple belief: that the world needs more moments of pure, unhurried joy. We set out to create an ice cream experience that honors traditional techniques while embracing modern, bold flavors. Every batch we produce is a labor of love, slow-churned to achieve a texture that is impossibly smooth and rich.

We partner with local dairy farmers to ensure our base is always fresh, and we source our mix-ins from artisans who care as much about quality as we do. From our kitchen to your cone, we are dedicated to providing a treat that helps you hit pause and just chill.
